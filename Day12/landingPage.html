Mobile App Landing Page for Sketch

This is a Mobile App Landing Page design template for presenting mobile apps on the Internet. Use it to design a nice landing page for your own app, or use it to present your clients’ apps. Easily change the app icon, device screenshots and header background image via the Sketch Symbols and Overrides. Use the design as it is, or customise for your apps brand by changing colors, fonts and more. The template is designed for the Bootstrap 4 Grid System, which is also included.
